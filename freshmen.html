Welcome, Freshmen!




Who we are


We are about genuine community, having fun, and being a place for everyone. We're a non-denominational campus ministry called CCF at Georgia Tech. We are more than just a name and more than just another house on campus. We aren’t a place. We aren’t a program. We are everywhere on campus. We’re a diverse group and we welcome people of different backgrounds and beliefs. We live together, serve together, eat, grow and explore together. We are a community of people who want good for everyone around us. Welcome to Tech! We'd love to meet you and be your friend.


What we do


Free food for freshmen every Monday, intramural teams, movie nights, off campus adventures, game nights, study groups, weekend trips, dance parties, late night waffle house runs. Opportunities to grow physically, mentally and spiritually, all while creating best friends for life.
