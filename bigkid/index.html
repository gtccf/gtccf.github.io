Big Kid on Campus is a day of fun at CCF and on Georgia Tech's campus for kids, siblings, sons, daughters, & grandchildren affected by cancer. We will have games, video games, a bounce house, crafts, makeovers, snacks, lunch, movies, and a Santa appearance. We also plan to play kickball with the GT Softball Team and have gym time with the GT Volleyball Team. Lunch is provided and the event is no cost to you! Feel free to drop off kids for a day of relaxation or Christmas shopping, or stay and hang out in our parents' lounge upstairs with internet access, TV, and snacks. We will have lots of adult volunteers - a ratio of at least 1 volunteer for every kid. A registered nurse will be present all day. We will serve Chick-Fil-A for lunch and have plenty to go around. Kids can come for all or part of the day. Feel free to invite friends using this evite link or else they can contact Marc Smith at marc@gtccf.org or just show up!

You may park directly behind CCF (not on the hill, but in the lot touching the back of the building). We will have people guiding you. You may enter our through the front glass door or the back double doors. The doors open directly into our activity room. It's easy.

Directions (GPS should work really well):
Address: 767 Techwood Dr. NW, Atlanta, GA 30313

From the North:
Take the Downtown Connector (I-75/I-85) South to the 14th/10th Street exit. Continue straight and turn right onto 10th Street. Take the first left at the light onto Fowler Street at McCamish Pavilion (basketball arena) and continue for about three blocks. At the first light, turn left onto 5th Street, then right at the first light onto Techwood Drive. CCF will be on the left after two blocks. CCF is at the corner of 4th Street and Techwood. There is a vertical, blue, wooden "ccf" sign in the front yard, along with two picnic tables and a wooden swing.

From the South:
Take the Downtown Connector (I-75/I-85) North to the Spring Street exit. Off the Exit, go through the first light (over Spring Street) and turn left up the hill at the next light onto West Peachtree Street. In one block turn left onto North Avenue. Traveling on North Ave., cross Spring Street, pass the Varsity on your right, and go over the bridge. Turn right onto Techwood Drive at the light immediately before the GT football stadium. CCF will be on the right in about four blocks. CCF is at the corner of 4th Street and Techwood. There is a vertical, blue, wooden "ccf" sign in the front yard, along with two picnic tables and a wooden swing.
