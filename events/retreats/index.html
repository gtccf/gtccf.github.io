Each semester, students have the opportunity to attend several CCF retreats. Retreats are a great opportunity to get away from busy Georgia Tech campus life, spend quality time with friends, invest in new and old relationships, and have a lot of fun! Read below for more information on retreats. Sign up with an intern at any CCF event or fill out this form, and check out our packing list before you go!
