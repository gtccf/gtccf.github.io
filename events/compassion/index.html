Each week, students can volunteer their time and talents through compassion events with CCF. There are several, weekly service opportunities listed below, as well as other compassion events and mission trips throughout each semester! Read below to find out about weekly service opportunities in the Atlanta area, and check out the Compassion page for more information on service trips, compassion weeks, and other annual events!



Mondays

3:00-4:30 | Making Sandwiches at CCF

On Mondays, we meet at the CCF house and make sandwiches for our friends on the streets.

For more information, contact James Mateyk or Rebecca Seasholtz.


4:30-6:00 | Bright Futures Team Building

Come hang out with us as we play leadership games with children at Bright Futures Academy and show them how to impact the future of Atlanta.

For more information, contact Denee Badois or Bryan Terschan.




Tuesdays

3:00-4:30 | Tutoring at City of Refuge

By tutoring at CORE Academy (City of Refuge Education), you get an opportunity to invest in elementary school students through helping with homework, reading stories, and playing games with the kids.

For more information, contact Marc Smith.


6:30 - 8:00 | Dinner at Wachovia Park

Help to cook a meal with us at CCF to share with our friends at Wachovia Park. By sharing a weekly dinner with our neighbors from the street, we work to show love to all those around us.

For more information, contact Locksly Walford or Paige Hoerle.




Wednesdays

12:00-2:00 | Knit-A-Square

Join us as we knit or crochet small squares to make blankets for orphans and vulnerable children in South Africa. Many of these children are suffering with HIV, which makes staying warm even more crucial for their health. Don’t know how to knit? No problem; we’ll teach you!

For more information, contact Austin Jones or Cari Gregg.


2:00-3:20 & 3:10-4:30 | Tutoring at Cristo Rey

By tutoring at Cristo Rey Atlanta Jesuit High School, you get an opportunity to invest in high school students through helping with homework and getting to make new friends within our Atlanta community.

For more information, contact Austin Jones or Emily Kruse.


3:30-5:00 | Bearings Bike Shop

Join us as we help children learn to fix bikes and encourage them as they learn the skill and value of hard work!

For more information, contact James Mateyk or Michael Worrell.




Thursdays

11:30-1:00 | ReMerge & Art

Every Thursday, we head over at noon to ReMerge (formerly known as Church on the Street) to share lunch and build relationships. Through sharing a meal with our most vulnerable neighbors, the homeless of Atlanta, we foster intentional and diverse Christian communities where everyone is welcome.

For more information, contact Kimberly Brown or Geoff Gilbert.
