Each week, CCF offers several events for all students. Read below for more information on event days and times. You're always welcome to bring a friend along!







Bakery | Tuesdays, 2-4 pm


The Bakery takes place each Tuesday at GTCCF from 2-4 pm throughout the fall and spring semesters. Our goal is to create a fun and personable environment where someone here for the 1st time to our most involved student can meet new people and enjoy a FREE treat and drink together.






Thursday Night Cafe | Thursdays, Dinner 7:15 pm, Service 8 pm


Come join CCF in person each Thursday night for dinner and/or a talk about what it means to follow Christ led by one of our very own campus ministers, Jonathan "Boo" Powell, Jordan Gignilliat, or Heather Powell, plus some worship led by the CCF band.
