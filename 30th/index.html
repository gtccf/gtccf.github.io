    • RSVP and Buy Banquet Tickets Here: https://gtccf-30th-anniversary.eventbrite.com

    If you mail a check, please then also email Ashley Latham with the Subject: RSVP for 30th Banquet and include the following information:

    • Which events you're attending:

      • Friday at Rick's
      • Saturday Banquet
      • Sunday Worship

    There will be a CCF memories slideshow during the banquet and we need your help to compile photos! Follow this link to visit the GTCCF 30th Flickr group to upload your favorite CCF memory photos: https://www.flickr.com/groups/gtccf30th/


    Childcare for the Banquet
    Childcare is provided on Saturday night at the CCF House. You can sign your kids in at 6:00pm before heading around the corner to the GA Tech Hotel & Conference Center. Please remember to bring along anything your kids might need such as diapers, bottles, change of clothes, snacks and any allergy medicine/information. There will be plenty of experienced caregivers ready to hang out with your kids!


    Parking for the Banquet
    A parking deck, attached to the Georgia Tech Hotel and Conference Center, offers easy and convenient parking. It's accessible from Spring Street and hourly rates are from $1.50 (for less than an hour) to $15 for the day. The parking garage address is 770 Spring St. NW, Atlanta, GA 30308-1031. Limited metered street parking is also available.
    For more detailed directions to the deck visit: https://www.scheller.gatech.edu/why-scheller/visit-campus/directions-parking.html
