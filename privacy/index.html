--- title: CCF Guest Internet Privacy Statement layout: default ---

What information do we collect?
Just your name, email and age.

How do we use the information?
Mainly to track network metrics, verify users are valid guests (and not any lurkers....), occasionally to confirm CCF mailing lists (no emails without permission).

What information do you share?
We don't share our data with any outside organizations. Only CCF staff see this list, if they remember to look at it.
