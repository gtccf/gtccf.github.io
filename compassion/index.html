We're a group of passionate and determined students who want to make a difference in the world. Together, we work to serve our community and everyone around us by giving our time and talents to help others. We do this by participating in weekly service events and trips that take place throughout the U.S. and around the world. See below for information about annual events and trips, and check out the Weekly Compassion Events page for information on weekly, Altanta-area service opportunities through CCF.






Juarez, Mexico Spring Break | March 21-25, 2016 (Spring Break)


This Spring Break, give someone the gift of a new home. Come to Juarez, Mexico to join CCF Compassion as we work with an organization named Casas por Cristo to build house for people in need.



Poland Service Trip | May 8-22, 2016


We will be doing various community service projects including home repair and kids camps. We will be working with the PROeM Ministries outside of Warsaw, Poland in Inowłódz. Check out pictures from 2013's Ecuador trip here.

Meet the Compassion Staff


CCF is run by sixteen interns who rotate each school year, and work closely with the ministry's eight full-time, adult staff members. We have three interns and one staff member dedicated to CCF's compassion events and ministry. Read below to learn all about the compassion staff, and visit the staff page to learn about the rest of the staff.





Marc Smith | Associate Minister | marc@gtccf.org


Marc Smith graduated from Georgia Tech with a degree in Mechanical Engineering in 2002. The following year he interned at GTCCF, after which he married his wife Leslie. Marc and Leslie moved to TN, where Marc earned his Masters of Divinity from Emmanuel School of Religion. Marc has worked in student ministry since 2006. In 2008 God blessed Marc & Leslie with their son Gabriel. In 2011 Marc returned to GTCCF. In January of 2012 Marc & Leslie welcomed their second boy, Liam, into the world! Marc helps students fight poverty in Atlanta and around the world.




Matthew Kinnemore | Associate Minister | matt@gtccf.org
