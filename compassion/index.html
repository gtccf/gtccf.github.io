We're a group of passionate and determined students who want to make a difference in the world. Together, we work to serve our community and everyone around us by giving our time and talents to help others. We do this by participating in weekly service events and trips that take place throughout the U.S. and around the world. See below for information about annual events and trips, and check out the Weekly Compassion Events page for information on weekly, Altanta-area service opportunities through CCF.




Big Kid on Campus | December 6, 2014


On Saturday, December 6, CCF Compassion will be hosting Big Kid on Campus, a day of fun for children that have in some way been affected by cancer. The day will be filled with fun entertainment and activities including games, crafts, makeovers, kickball with a Georgia Tech sports team, and even an appearance by Santa! Talk to Marc Smith for more information about how to participate or sign up!

Restoration Atlanta | January 10, 2015


On Saturday, January 10th, join CCF as we volunteer to serve our community. Restoration Atlanta is an incredible way to give back to our city. We are meeting at CCF at 9am, where we'll serve you a hot breakfast. After that, you'll be given the choice to volunteer at one of the seven opportunities to serve throughout Atlanta.



ARGO: Fall Break Service Trip | October 10-14 (Fall Break)


Come with us to serve in the cities of Mayflower and Vilonia, Arkansas as we work to assist in tornado relief. We are going to help the people of Arkansas as they work to recover and rebuild. Cost is $100. We will leave CCF at 7PM on Friday evening and be back by 8PM Tuesday. Sign up here or talk to Marc Smith for more info.



Clean Water Trip | December 27 - January 2


Embrace the New Year in Puebla, Mexico as we work with the locals to help build a well for the town. Check out pictures from last year's Puebla trip here.



Week of Compassion | March 2-7


CCF spends a week focusing on service. Join us for Café Unplugged on March 3rd and Compassion Thursday on March 5th. Also come out to serve around Atlanta through the CCF Compassion weekly events.



Juarez, Mexico Spring Break | March 16-20 (Spring Break)


This Spring Break, give someone the gift of a new home. Come to Juarez, Mexico to join CCF Compassion as we work with an organization named Casas por Cristo to build house for people in need.



Poland Service Trip | May 2015


We will be doing various community service projects including home repair and kids camps. We will be working with the PROeM Ministries outside of Warsaw, Poland in Inowłódz. Check out pictures from last year's Ecuador trip here.



Living Water International Student/Alumni Trip | August 23-29


Spend a week in El Salvador as CCF Compassion works with Living Water International to show the love of Christ by bring clean water to a town. We'll spend a week digging and building a well with the locals.

Meet the Compassion Staff


CCF is run by sixteen interns who rotate each school year, and work closely with the ministry's eight full-time, adult staff members. We have three interns and one staff member dedicated to CCF's compassion events and ministry. Read below to learn all about the compassion staff, and visit the staff page to learn about the rest of the staff.





Marc Smith | Associate Minister | marc@gtccf.org


Marc Smith graduated from Georgia Tech with a degree in Mechanical Engineering in 2002. The following year he interned at GTCCF, after which he married his wife Leslie. Marc and Leslie moved to TN, where Marc earned his Masters of Divinity from Emmanuel School of Religion. Marc has worked in student ministry since 2006. In 2008 God blessed Marc & Leslie with their son Gabriel. In 2011 Marc returned to GTCCF. In January of 2012 Marc & Leslie welcomed their second boy, Liam, into the world! Marc helps students fight poverty in Atlanta and around the world.
