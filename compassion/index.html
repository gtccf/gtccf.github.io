We're a group of passionate and determined students who want to make a difference in the world. Together, we work to serve our community and everyone around us by giving our time and talents to help others. We do this by participating in weekly service events and trips that take place throughout the U.S. and around the world. See below for information about annual events and trips.




Weekly Compassion Events


Every week during the school year, we go out into the community and serve. Check out the Weekly Compassion Events page for information on weekly, Altanta-area service opportunities through CCF.



Go West: Fall Break Service Trip | October 8-11, 2016 (Fall Break)


Come "Dam the Flood" with us and serve in Rainelle, West Virginia as we work to assist in flood relief. We are going to help the people of West Virginia as they work to recover and rebuild from the recent floods there. Cost is $100. We will leave CCF at 6:00AM on Saturday morning and be back on Tuesday evening. Talk to Marc Smith for more info.



Big Kid on Campus | December 10, 2016


On Saturday, December 10, CCF Compassion will be hosting Big Kid on Campus, a day of fun for children that have in some way been affected by cancer. The day will be filled with fun entertainment and activities including games, crafts, makeovers, kickball with a Georgia Tech sports team, and even an appearance by Santa!

If you are interested in volunteering to make this happen and spend the day with these wonderful kiddos, you are also welcome! We need student volunteers at each and every event and would love to have YOU. So, if you so desire, come to as much as you are able to throughout the day!



Clean Water Trip | December 30, 2016 - January 6, 2017


Embrace the New Year in Puebla, Mexico as we work with the locals to help build a well for the town. Check out pictures from last year's Puebla trip here and here.



Restoration Atlanta | January 28, 2017


Join CCF as we volunteer to serve our community. Restoration Atlanta is an incredible way to give back to our city. We are meeting at CCF at 9am, where we'll serve you a hot breakfast. After that, you'll be given the choice to volunteer at one of the seven opportunities to serve throughout Atlanta.




Juarez, Mexico Spring Break | March 18-26, 2017 (Spring Break)


This Spring Break, give someone the gift of a new home. Come to Juarez, Mexico to join CCF Compassion as we work with an organization named Casas por Cristo to build house for people in need.



Poland Service Trip | May 7-21, 2017


We will be doing various community service projects including home repair and kids camps. We will be working with the PROeM Ministries outside of Warsaw, Poland in Inowłódz. There is the choice of attending the first week, the second week, or both weeks. For more information, check this form or email Marc Smith

Meet the Compassion Staff


CCF is run by a group of interns who rotate each school year, and work closely with the ministry's eight full-time, adult staff members. We have three interns and one staff member dedicated to CCF's compassion events and ministry. Read below to learn all about the compassion staff, and visit the staff page to learn about the rest of the staff.





Marc Smith | Associate Minister | marc@gtccf.org


Marc Smith graduated from Georgia Tech with a degree in Mechanical Engineering in 2002. The following year he interned at GTCCF, after which he married his wife Leslie. Marc and Leslie moved to TN, where Marc earned his Masters of Divinity from Emmanuel Christian Seminary. Marc has worked in student ministry since 2006. In 2011 Marc returned to GTCCF. Marc helps students fight poverty in Atlanta and around the world. God has blessed Marc & Leslie with 3 young children.
