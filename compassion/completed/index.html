Here you can find out about the trips that we have already gone to this year to see what we've done, and see if you'd like to join us next year!


Completed Service Trips




Hustle II Houston
Houston, TX | October 5-9, 2018

Over fall break, we worked to bring relief to the victims of Hurricane Harvey! We had an amazing time helping with hurrican debris cleanup and helping local people get back on their feet. The trip was lead by Marc Smith, so please contact him if you would like to know more!




Winter Break: Clean Water
Puebla, Mexico | Dec. 30, 2018 - Jan. 6, 2019

We embraced the New Year in Puebla, Mexico as we worked with the locals to help build a well for the surrounding community. Photos from this trip will be coming soon! If you have any questions about this trip, you can contact Marc Smith.

Completed Campus Events




Restoration Atlanta
August 2018

In august we volunteered to serve our community all over the city! Restoration Atlanta is an incredible way to give back to our city be being able to volunteer at one of several opportunities to serve throughout Atlanta. Join us again this Spring!


Big Kid on Campus
December 8, 2018

In December we hosted Big Kid on Campus, a day of fun for children that have in some way been affected by cancer. The day was filled with fun entertainment and activities including games, crafts, makeovers, kickball with a Georgia Tech sports team, and even an appearance by Santa!
