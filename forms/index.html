CCF has alot of signups for alot of things. Here is a list of the available forms that you can use to get involved, get connected and hangout with us at specific events.



Life Groups

Lifegroups are small groups of 2-4 people your own age and gender that meet once a week with a leader to discuss life and spirituality.

Small Groups

Students have the opportunity to join various CCF small groups. There are "life groups" offered for students to meet weekly with an intern and several other students, and there are other small groups dedicated to certain topics. Checkout current offerings here.

Weekly Compassion Events

CCF each week as opportunities to help out in the community around Atlanta. Fill out this form if you want to learn more! Read more here.

CCF Service Trip Interest

If you want to take a trip that does some good in the world, consider a CCF service trip. Read more here and if you are interested, fill out this form and someone will get back to you!

Day Of Worship

Come join CCF as we take a day to focus on ways that we connect with God as individuals and a community.

Prayerbox

Each week, interns and student leaders gather to pray for the CCF and GaTech community. Submit a prayer request here and someone will pray for it throughout the semester.

Mugshot

Have a comment about Cafe? A prayer request related to the Cafe Talk? Type out a response for Mugshot, and he'll communicate it to the Cafe Team
