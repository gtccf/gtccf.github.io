CCF has a lot of signups for a lot of things! Here is a list of the available forms that you can use to get involved, get connected and hangout with us at specific events.



Life Groups

Lifegroups are small groups of 2-4 people your own age and gender that meet once a week with a leader to discuss life and faith. Read more or sign up with interest here.

Small Groups

There are small groups dedicated to certain topics, what it means to follow Jesus, and even general biblical knowledge. "Life groups" are offered for students to meet weekly with an intern and several other students to explore faith and what it means to you. Read below for more information. Times and dates vary by semester. If you see something you like, sign up for a small group here.

Our Next Retreat

Retreats are a great time for students to take a small break from school and get away from campus to recharge with friends. If you want to learn more about our next retreat, click here.

Weekly Compassion Events

CCF each week has opportunities to help out in the community around Atlanta. Fill out this form if you want to learn more! Read more here, or sign up with interest here.

CCF Service Trip Interest

If you want to take a trip that does some good in the world, consider a CCF service trip. Read more here and if you are interested, fill out this form and someone will get back to you!

Prayer

Each week, interns and student leaders gather to pray for the CCF and GaTech community. Submit a prayer request here and someone will pray for it throughout the semester.
